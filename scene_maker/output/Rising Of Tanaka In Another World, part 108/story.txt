Rising Of Tanaka In Another World, part 108.

Tanaka receives shocking news about General White's death. He stands in disbelief, overwhelmed by the unexpected turn of events.

Tanaka uses his powerful magic technique, Absolute Overdrive Zero Formula, to track Warden Muchisuki through the movement of air. His friends, Yashichi and Toby, watch in awe as he demonstrates his mastery over the element.

The trio chases after Muchisuki through a long staircase, reaching an execution hall where General Red awaits them. Kingdom soldiers surround them, preparing to attack.

General Red orders the kingdom soldiers to capture Tanaka and his companions. Yashichi steps forward, showcasing his incredible strength and combat skills in a desperate attempt to protect them.

Tanaka reflects on his friendship with General White, remembering their brief but meaningful time together. His determination to honor his friend's memory fuels his resolve to continue fighting.
