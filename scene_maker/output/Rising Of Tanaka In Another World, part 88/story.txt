Rising Of Tanaka In Another World, part 88.

Murgai contemplates his loyalty and jealousy toward Baron Almond, struggling with his opposition to the alliance.

The horned tribe gathers in the plaza, anxious about the missing barrier. Baron Almond approaches Patriarch, hinting at a hidden agenda.

Baron Almond reveals his scheme, presenting the horns of the horned tribe's ancestors as evidence of their wealth potential. The Patriarch grows enraged.

Shijimi confronts Baron Almond, pleading for her clan's safety. Baron Almond mocks her loyalty and orders her execution to silence the horned tribe.

The Patriarch realizes his foolishness in rushing the event for his son's succession, as his son opposes him. The clan members are restrained by Baron Almond's soldiers.
