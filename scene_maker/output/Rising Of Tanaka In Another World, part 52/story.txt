Rising Of Tanaka In Another World, part 52.

Tanaka stands on a balcony overlooking the dark city, contemplating his situation and plotting to protect Aisa-chan from an impending threat.

Tanaka spots a black-clad figure sneaking into the pavilion. Recognizing him as a threat, Tanaka confronts the intruder with confidence and skill, showcasing his determination to protect Aisa-chan.

The confrontation escalates as Tanaka uses invisible magic to incapacitate the assassin. He escapes through a hole in the ceiling, narrowly avoiding detection by guards led by Taisa.

Aisa-chan, caught in the chaos, hides herself and expresses confusion about the sudden turn of events. The soldiers arrive, investigating the incident and tightening security at the pavilion.
