Rising Of Tanaka In Another World, part 159.

Tanaka confidently enters the guild room, having regained his spirits after a recent setback. He approaches Echigoya with a proposition to address the town's food problem.

Echigoya, wary of Tanaka's intentions but intrigued by his confidence, engages in conversation about the town's challenges. Tanaka outlines his plan to use agriculture and magic to solve the food shortage.

Tanaka details his agricultural strategy, explaining how he will utilize magic to water crops and abundant land resources to ensure a bountiful harvest.

Echigoya, cautious but seeing potential in Tanaka's proposal, agrees to let him proceed. Tanaka departs with renewed confidence in his ability to make an impact.

Tanaka reflects on his plans, imagining a successful future where he becomes a respected leader in the town, thanks to his agricultural success.
