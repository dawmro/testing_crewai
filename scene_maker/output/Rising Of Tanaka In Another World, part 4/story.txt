Rising Of Tanaka In Another World, part 4.

Tanaka, filled with excitement, approaches the General with questions about his new reality. The General explains the concept of contracts with the God of Creation, setting the stage for Tanaka's journey.

The General reveals that Tanaka's ability to communicate and exist in this world is proof of his contract. He expresses disbelief at Earth's environment, highlighting the uniqueness of this new world.

Tanaka's excitement reaches a peak as he eagerly asks the General how to access his personal information. The General guides him, leading to Tanaka's attempt to focus and access his stats.

Tanaka, brimming with enthusiasm, performs a quirky incantation to access his stats. His chuunibyou tendencies shine through as he speaks dramatically, setting up the comedic tone of the scene.

The General reacts with exasperation as Tanaka's stats appear, revealing surprisingly low values. The scene captures the disappointment and humor as Tanaka realizes his weak stats.

Tanaka, disheartened by his stats, questions his role in this world. The General explains that heroes typically have higher stats, comparing them to Tanaka's low numbers and highlighting the stark contrast.
