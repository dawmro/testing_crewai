Rising Of Tanaka In Another World, part 197.

Kamui is summoned as the Hero and struggles against Princess Lala's control. He feels immense pain as Morinaga begins to possess his body.

Yashichi arrives with Mikoto, using divine power to stop Morinaga's advance. The light from Yashichi's hands causes Princess Lala and her apostles to falter.

The battle begins as the apostles of Morinaga attack Kamui. Mikoto intervenes with a surprise dropkick, creating an opening for Kamui to escape.

Kamui stands against the odds, determined to fight alongside Yashichi and Tetsujin. He realizes his true role as a Hero and prepares to battle Morinaga's forces.
