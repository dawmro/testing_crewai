Rising Of Tanaka In Another World, part 200.

Morinaga traps Tanaka in multi-layered barriers using immense energy, showcasing her overwhelming power.

Tanaka attempts to defuse the tension with his signature charm and 'Finger Snap' technique, but it backfires spectacularly.

Morinaga's fury intensifies as she unleashes a powerful attack resembling the 'Crevice of Worlds,' overwhelming Tanaka.

Tanaka struggles to resist the darkness but ultimately fails, being pulled into it despite his desperate plea for help.

Yashichi's unwavering determination and hope inspire others, as they prepare to face the unknown fate of Tanaka and the world.
